About

Afrobeat is a music genre which involves the comibantion of elements of West African musical styles such as fuji music and highlife with American Jazz and later soul and funk influences.

The term was coined in the 1960s by Nigerian multi-instrumentalist and bandleader Fela Kuti who is responsible for pioneering and popularizing the style both within and outside Nigeria.

Destinct from Afrobeat is Afrobeats- a sound originating in West Africa in the 21st Century which takes in diverse influence and is an electric combination of genres such as azonto, dancehall, highlife, British, hiphop,Naija beats, ndomobolo, Rnb etc.
